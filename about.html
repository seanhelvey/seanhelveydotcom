About

I create and maintain software that helps social workers at Koinonia Family Services. The tools I currently use are Python, Django, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP). I am passionate about surfing, organizing the Central Coast Python Meetup, and reading (follow me on Goodreads).

My heartfelt yoga classes incorporate mindfulness, breathwork, and ecstatic dance. I encourage students of all shapes and sizes to feel at home in their body, noticing emotions underlying physical alignment and posture. I completed a 200 hour teacher training at Yoga Soup where I continue to serve as a Shine Ambassador.
